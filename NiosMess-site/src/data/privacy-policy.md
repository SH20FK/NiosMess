## NiosMess Privacy Policy

This privacy notice explains how NiosMess collects and processes information when you use our apps and services.

### What we collect
- Account data (username, email, public profile fields)
- Messages and media you send through the service
- Device and session metadata (IP, device name, last activity)

### How we use data
- To deliver messages and media
- To keep your account secure
- To improve performance and reliability

### Your choices
- You can update your profile at any time
- You can request deletion of your account and data

### Contact
If you have questions, contact: support@niosmess.app

This policy can be updated as the service evolves.
