PRIVACY AND PERSONAL DATA PROCESSING POLICY OF THE "NIOSMESS" SERVICE

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") defines the procedure for processing and protecting personal data of users of the NiosMess service (hereinafter referred to as the "Service", "Messenger") carried out by the Service Administration (hereinafter referred to as the "Operator") in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Terms and Definitions
Operator — The Administration of the NiosMess Service, which independently or jointly with other parties organizes and carries out the processing of personal data.
User — Any individual registered in the Messenger and using its features.
Personal Data (PD) — Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (data subject).
End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) — A data transmission method where only the Users participating in the communication have access to the messages. The Operator does not possess decryption keys and has no access to the content of such messages.

2. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data
The Operator processes Users' personal data based on the following legal grounds:
- The User's consent to the processing of their personal data, expressed by performing affirmative actions (checking the tick-box during registration).
- Performance of the Terms of Service to which the User is a party.
- Legitimate interests of the Operator (such as fraud prevention, ensuring Service security and stability).

3. List of Processed Personal Data
The Operator processes the following data of the User:
- Email address;
- Username and Display Name;
- Profile description ("About Me" / Bio);
- Profile image (Avatar);
- Network identifiers: IP address, device type and model, operating system, cookies, and local storage technologies (LocalStorage, IndexedDB) for session analytics and security purposes;
- Files and media voluntarily uploaded by the User to public channels, groups, or chats without end-to-end encryption.

Important Note on Secret Chats (E2EE):
The Operator processes Secret Chat messages exclusively in encrypted form (as unreadable encrypted data blocks) and transfers them between Users' devices. Decryption keys are stored solely locally on the Users' devices. Due to the architecture of E2EE, the Operator has no technical ability to decrypt, read, modify, or store the contents of Secret Chats in an unencrypted format.

4. Purposes of Processing Personal Data
Personal data of Users is processed solely for the following purposes:
- Registration and identification of the User within the Service;
- Providing the core functionality of the messenger (message delivery, creation of groups and channels, publishing posts in the NiosGram feed);
- Ensuring account security, including sending two-factor authentication (2FA) codes;
- Moderation of public content in accordance with the Terms of Service (ToS) and applicable laws;
- Technical support and handling inquiries in case of account freeze.

5. Collection, Storage, and Deletion of Personal Data
5.1. Data Location: The Operator hosts and processes the personal data of Users on secure servers located in the Netherlands (European Union) in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
5.2. Personal data is collected directly when the User fills out registration forms and during the use of the Messenger.
5.3. Personal data is stored in a form that allows identification of the data subject for no longer than is necessary for the purposes of its processing.
5.4. Upon account deletion by the User or upon a valid erasure request, all personal data of the User is permanently deleted or anonymized within 30 calendar days, except where a longer retention period is required by applicable law.

6. Disclosure of Personal Data to Third Parties and International Transfers
6.1. The Operator does not transfer personal data of Users to third parties without their explicit consent, except when necessary to comply with applicable law or to respond to lawful requests from law enforcement agencies.
6.2. Any international transfer of personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) is carried out in accordance with applicable laws, using Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other appropriate safeguards to ensure an adequate level of data protection.

7. Rights of Users (Data Subjects)
Under the GDPR, the User has the right:
- To access their personal data and receive a copy of the processed data;
- To request rectification, restriction, or erasure ("right to be forgotten") of their personal data if it is incomplete, outdated, inaccurate, or unlawfully obtained;
- To withdraw consent to the processing of personal data at any time by sending a request to the Operator. Withdrawal of consent results in the deletion of the Messenger account;
- To data portability;
- To object to the processing of their data;
- To lodge a complaint with a supervisory data protection authority.

8. Protection of Personal Data
The Operator takes necessary and sufficient legal, organizational, and technical measures to protect personal data from unauthorized or accidental access, destruction, alteration, blocking, copying, distribution, as well as from other unlawful actions of third parties in accordance with international information security standards.

9. Amendments to the Privacy Policy
The Operator has the right to make changes to this Policy. The new version of the Policy takes effect from the moment it is posted on the Service website, unless otherwise provided by the new version.

10. Contact Information
For inquiries, withdrawal of consent, or obtaining information regarding the processing of personal data, the User may contact the Operator via email at: niosmess@gmail.com.
